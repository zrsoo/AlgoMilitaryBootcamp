# Berlin Compensation & Living-Cost Model

## Purpose

This note captures the working model discussed for living in Berlin while treating stock as separate wealth.

The main framing is:

```text
Live from cash.
Treat stock as separate wealth accumulation.
Assume stock taxes are handled in-stock / sell-to-cover, so stock does not drain monthly cash.
```

## Tax / net-income assumptions

Assumptions used for the estimate:

- Berlin / Germany.
- Single person.
- Tax class I.
- No church tax.
- No children.
- Statutory public health insurance.
- Stock is taxed at vest as German employment income.
- Stock tax is assumed to be handled through stock withholding / sell-to-cover, not by draining monthly cash.
- Numbers are planning estimates, not tax-filing calculations.
